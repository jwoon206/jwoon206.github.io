About Me

Hello there! My name is Jessica Woon and I am currently a Junior at the University of Southern California majoring in Human Biology and double minoring in Health Care Studies and Web Technologies and Applications (such a mouthful!). I was born and raised in rainy Seattle, but have moved to sunny Los Angeles for college to explore new opportunities in a different environment. So far, I have really enjoyed my time in Los Angeles as I have met such a diverse body of students on campus and have gotten the chance to try some of the most delicious foods in Koreatown and Little Tokyo.
I have a passion for directly helping people, especially those in underserved communities, and plan on applying to medical school in the future to work in pediatrics. In the meantime, I strive to make the most out of my college experience by engaging in my hobbies and participating in extracurricular activities I am passionate about. This website will provide a glimpse of my life and some of the activities that make my life ten-times brighter.

I hope you enjoy learning a little more about myself!
