~Acai Bowls~


Acai Bowls are one of my favorite treats to make with my friends and whenever I am craving something sweet! These delicious treats are super easy to make by just adding acai puree, frozen fruits, fresh fruits, and water in a blender! Next, the best part is decorating my acai bowl by topping it off with fresh fruits, granola, and chia seeds. Whenever I make acai bowls, I always try to create new recipes by adding different fruits or liquid base into the blender, and topping if off with different fruits as well.
